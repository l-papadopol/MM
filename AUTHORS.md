## Development workflow

MadModem is developed using an AI-assisted coding workflow: the human author
drives the software as designer, tester, integrator and product owner, while AI
accelerates implementation work. This is a deliberate creative process intended
to multiply human intellectual capability and reduce the mechanical burden of
manual coding.

Scheduler

In the Scheduler tab you can type a Maidenhead locator, country/DXCC or prefix from cty.csv. MM computes bearing and distance from your QTH and points the active rotator.

The received table includes Bearing. FT TX remains under the UTC scheduler and is inhibited when station identity is missing or when the requested rotator is not ready.

Bright red MOV means rotator moving; bright green RDY means configured, connected and stopped. If no rotator is configured, both lamps are greyed.

Waterfall

The waterfall shows the selected mode audio passband. Left click moves RX; in FT modes right click moves TX. FT and RTTY contest callouts can be drawn over the trace.

The waterfall no longer uses a hover tooltip. Help is shown in the status bar and What’s This? mode, avoiding the large black rectangle on some Linux themes.

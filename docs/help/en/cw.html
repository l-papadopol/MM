CW / Morse

CW uses the integrated skimmer engine under the hood while keeping manual A/B tuning on the waterfall. Left click selects RX A, right click selects optional RX B. CW TX generates a clean sine with raised-cosine key shaping and a narrow transmit band-pass around the tone.

CW receive: CW RX uses the integrated skimmer engine. Left click sets the green RX A marker; right click sets the optional blue RX B marker.

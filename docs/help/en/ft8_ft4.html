FT4 / FT8

The received table includes Bearing. CQ rows keep CQ colouring, but the dashed red outline marks any decoded valid callsign that is not yet in the ADIF logbook, even when the station is inside another QSO rather than calling CQ. FT TX remains under the UTC scheduler and is inhibited when station identity is missing or when the requested rotator is not ready.

Mandatory station identity

Before moving the rotator or transmitting, configure a valid My Call and My Locator in Settings → User/QTH. If they are missing, MM blocks TX, PTT, Tune, calibration and rotator pointing.

Runtime log

The Runtime log button is shown here in the FT Mode tab, not in Status, so FT timing/CAT/PTT diagnostics stay close to FT controls.

Rotator

If TX or rotator movement does not start, first check My Call/My Locator, rotator endpoint separated from radio CAT, rotator connection, and MOV/RDY lamps.

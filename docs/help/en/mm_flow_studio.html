MM Flow Studio

Flow Studio uses the Event Bus and Capability Manager. Flow blocks do not directly drive PTT, audio, CAT or rotator: they request safe actions from real modules.

The received table includes Bearing. FT TX remains under the UTC scheduler and is inhibited when station identity is missing or when the requested rotator is not ready.
